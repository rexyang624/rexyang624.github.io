## Holstein-Primakoff transformation

For spin operators, we have

$$
\begin{align*}
    &S^+=\hbar\sqrt{2S}\sqrt{1-\frac{a^\dagger a}{2S}}a\\
    &S^-=\hbar\sqrt{2S}a^\dagger\sqrt{1-\frac{a^\dagger a}{2S}}\\
    &S^z\,=\hbar(S-a^\dagger a)
\end{align*}
$$
